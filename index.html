Introduction:

Each year, the cattle industry in the US hemorrhages over $100 billion due to treatment costs, lost revenue from sick cattle, antibiotic overuse, deaths, and greenhouse gas emissions. Beyond the US borders, the situation is equally critical, exacerbating the global impact of these challenges. The unpredictability of cattle sickness poses a significant challenge. By the time it's detected, the infection spreads throughout the ranch and contaminated products shipped out. This not only risks harm to consumers but also tarnishes the brand's reputation when recalls occur, impacting quality perception. To address this issue, many ranches administer antibiotics to all incoming calves, regardless of need, leading to antibiotic overuse, detrimental to human health. Additionally, sick animals emit more methane, exacerbating greenhouse gas emissions. Prolonged sickness in animals only amplifies methane production, compounding environmental concerns.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions to help you with your initial due diligence and information gathering:
How does MyAnIML tech work?

We take a picture or video of a cattle’s face. Our system analyzes the face and muzzle
(nose print) and measures any subtle changes. It uses our proprietary dataset to analyze and
measure changes and informs ranch manager.

Why muzzle?

Muzzle is very unique much like our fingerprints, and no two are alike. In our research, we have found that muzzle shows changes before cattle is symptomatic.

There are other companies providing disease information as well. How are you different from others?

While other companies detect sickness only after cattle are already infected and spreading disease, our approach predicts symptoms 2-3 days in advance, before disease transmission begins. By intervening earlier, we prevent disease spread, setting us apart in safeguarding animal health and preventing outbreaks.

How are you providing notification?

We send a notification via email with sick cattle’s ear tag number. We also provide Bluetooth tags with an app for the rancher to view the tag number in the app and go directly to the cattle instead of reading each tag to get to that soon to be sick cattle.

What diseases do you provide notifications on?

Currently we only do sick vs. healthy. We have just one disease pinkeye in our dataset with 99.4% accuracy, however we need another disease before we can start differentiating among diseases. We are working on BRD (bovine respiratory disease) dataset at this time. We are close to 70% accuracy and when we get to high 90s accuracy then we can start providing disease specific notifications.

Did you raise pre-seed funding?

Yes. We generated via SAFE note with $3M cap.

What will you do with the seed funding?

We need to develop our GTM product. Currently we are using GoPros on a feeder truck and it requires WiFi to upload videos. There are many ranches with no wifi and very little cellular coverage. Our GTM product will be an edge product that wouldn’t require any connectivity to the cloud. It will take pictures on the spot and provide notifications on the spot. It can be mounted on any place in a ranch such as water source, by the chute, and/or by the animal offloading area.

The seed fund will be spent to develop this product and hire installation and sales team to install and build more relationships.

Do you have any 3rd party validation?

Yes, we worked with USDA to develop validation of our tech. Please see the link above.

What is the long term vision?

In addition to BRD, we intend to go after Pasteurella, Estrous, Mastitis and Ketosis among others. We also intend to launch the product to other livestock animals.

How are you protecting your technology?

We are the only ones using face and muzzle to predict diseases. We have filed two patents that cover placing cameras at various locations and taking videos/pictures for health reasons. Both of these patents cover processes so no one else can use any other process to do the same.
